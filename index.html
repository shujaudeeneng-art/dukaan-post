Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Dukaan Post kya hai aur yeh kis tarah kaam karta hai?

Dukaan Post ek aasan marketing tool hai jo chhote shopkeepers ko unki shop ke mutabiq chand seconds mein social media posts aur WhatsApp status bana kar deta hai, wo bhi Urdu aur English dono zubano mein.

Kya Dukaan Post istemal karne ke paise lagte hain?

Nahi, Dukaan Post bilkul free hai. Koi signup, credit card, ya monthly subscription ki zaroorat nahi hai.

Agar main post mein koi discount offer nahi dena chahta toh kya karoon?

Aap simple Discount wale option mein "No Discount" select kar lein ya use khali chhor dein. Tool automatically aisi post banayega jisme discount ka zikr nahi hoga, aur aapka post bilkul professional lagega.

Kaunse business categories ke liye posts ban sakte hain?

Kapron ki dukaan, mobile shop, restaurant, bakery, cosmetics, electronics, grocery store, jewelry, footwear, aur kayi doosre business types ke liye special themes aur templates maujood hain.
